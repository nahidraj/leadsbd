What We Do

Sustainable Education

Livelihood Education And Development Services(LEADS) Foundation

LEADS provides quality & sustainable education & healthcare facilities through different awareness & education support programme, for the children especially underprivileged children enrolled in different government primary schools. LEADS mainly works towards development of the underprivileged section of the community with better and more effective learning process in existing government curriculum, while developing their social awareness, basic moral values and change their behaviour.
